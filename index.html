About me

I'm a second year computer science graduate student at the University of Groningen and a core developer of Flask Monitoring Dashboard. I'm currently (April 2019) working on my master's thesis, called Measuring the Performance of a Performance Monitor. The case of the Flask Monitoring Dashboard.

Bio

Since September 2017, I've been a graduate student in computing science at the University of Groningen, in the Software Engineering and Distributed Systems track. Apart from successfully completing all my courses, I've also worked as a teaching assistant for Parallel Computing, Web and cloud Computing, and Software Maintenance and Evolution.

Between 2013 and 2017, I studied at the Faculty of Automatic Control and Computers, University Politehnica of Bucharest, where I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering degree, with a final average of 9.In the second half of my studies, I worked part time as a software engineer for Tellence and Kepler-Rominfo, two Bucharest-based IT companies.
